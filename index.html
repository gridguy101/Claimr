The Problem

Airdrops are vulnerable. Public contract addresses let bots and snipers manipulate markets before tokens even launch, creating unfair outcomes and harming community trust.

The Solution

FairDrop introduces a private, controlled pre-release airdrop marketplace. Trade voucher rights to future tokens without revealing the contract address. This ensures a sniper-proof, transparent launch, letting true supporters and investors discover price fairly before the on-chain debut.

Our Roadmap

Q1: Initial Development

Craft a world-class UI/UX, build the voucher trading engine, and implement secure hashed commitments to keep the contract secret pre-launch.

Q2: Beta Testing

Invite select projects and communities to test the pre-release marketplace. Gather feedback, refine the user experience, and ensure seamless functionality.

Q3: Public Launch

Go live, supporting major projects. Host airdrops with transparent, fair pre-launch price discovery without bot interference.

Q4: Expansion

Form strategic partnerships, integrate with major exchanges, and continually improve security, trust, and community engagement.
